# Clyros Tech Private Limited
## Operating Metrics & Controls Snapshot

**Document Classification:** Internal Only – Management Use  
**Audience:** Executive Leadership, Board of Directors, Investors  
**Purpose:** Define operating metrics structure, controls, and accountability  
**Last Updated:** January 2026

---

## Purpose of This Document

This document describes the **structure** and **categories** of operating metrics used to monitor business health, delivery quality, and risk. It does NOT contain actual data or numbers—those are reported separately in operating reviews.

This framework supports:
- Management decision-making and operational oversight
- Board review of business performance and risk
- Investor diligence on management discipline and controls

---

## 1. Delivery Performance Metrics

### 1.1 Project Execution

**Metrics:**
- **On-Time Delivery Rate:** Percentage of projects completed within committed timeline
- **On-Budget Delivery Rate:** Percentage of fixed-price projects completed within budget
- **Scope Change Frequency:** Number of formal scope changes per project
- **Client Escalations:** Number of escalations to executive leadership per engagement

**Purpose:**  
Assess delivery discipline, project management effectiveness, and client satisfaction.

**Review Cadence:**  
- Real-time monitoring by Delivery Leadership
- Monthly rollup review by Executive Leadership
- Quarterly board reporting

**Ownership:**  
- **Primary:** Delivery Manager (per engagement)
- **Rollup:** Delivery Leadership
- **Oversight:** Executive Leadership / Board

**Targets:**  
(Targets would be defined based on business maturity and client expectations. Example: >85% on-time delivery, <10% client escalations)

### 1.2 Quality & Defects

**Metrics:**
- **Production Defects:** Critical/high-severity issues discovered post-deployment
- **Security Vulnerabilities:** Vulnerabilities identified in security scans or audits
- **Code Review Coverage:** Percentage of code changes reviewed before merge
- **Automated Test Coverage:** Percentage of codebase covered by automated tests

**Purpose:**  
Monitor technical quality and reduce post-deployment risk.

**Review Cadence:**  
- Real-time monitoring by engineering teams
- Weekly review by Delivery Leadership
- Monthly executive review
- Quarterly board reporting (summary)

**Ownership:**  
- **Primary:** Technical Lead (per engagement)
- **Rollup:** Delivery Leadership
- **Oversight:** Executive Leadership

**Targets:**  
(Example: <5 critical defects per quarter, >80% code review coverage)

### 1.3 Client Satisfaction

**Metrics:**
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS):** Client likelihood to recommend (if surveyed)
- **Client Retention Rate:** Percentage of clients with repeat engagements
- **Contract Renewal Rate:** Percentage of retainer/dedicated team contracts renewed
- **Client Feedback Themes:** Qualitative themes from retrospectives and check-ins

**Purpose:**  
Measure client satisfaction and likelihood of repeat business.

**Review Cadence:**  
- Post-engagement surveys (if implemented)
- Quarterly client retention analysis
- Annual board review

**Ownership:**  
- **Primary:** Business Development / Account Management
- **Rollup:** Executive Leadership
- **Oversight:** Board

**Targets:**  
(Example: >70% client retention, positive NPS if surveyed)

## 2. Financial & Operating Efficiency

### 2.1 Revenue & Billing

**Metrics:**
- **Monthly/Quarterly Revenue:** Recognized revenue per period
- **Revenue Growth Rate:** Quarter-over-quarter or year-over-year growth
- **Contract Value Backlog:** Value of signed but not yet delivered engagements
- **Average Deal Size:** Mean contract value (by engagement type)

**Purpose:**  
Track top-line growth and pipeline health.

**Review Cadence:**  
- Monthly financial close
- Quarterly board reporting
- Annual strategic planning

**Ownership:**  
- **Primary:** Finance (or Executive if no CFO)
- **Oversight:** Board

**Targets:**  
(Revenue targets aligned with strategic plan and hiring capacity)

### 2.2 Utilization & Productivity

**Metrics:**
- **Utilization Rate:** Billable hours as percentage of available capacity
- **Realization Rate:** Actual billed amount vs. standard rates
- **Revenue Per Engineer:** Total revenue divided by engineering headcount
- **Bench Time:** Non-billable time between engagements (per engineer)

**Purpose:**  
Measure operational efficiency and capacity management.

**Review Cadence:**  
- Weekly utilization tracking by Delivery Leadership
- Monthly executive review
- Quarterly board reporting

**Ownership:**  
- **Primary:** Delivery Leadership
- **Rollup:** Executive Leadership / Finance
- **Oversight:** Board

**Targets:**  
(Example: 70-80% utilization, >90% realization)

### 2.3 Profitability

**Metrics:**
- **Gross Margin:** Revenue minus direct labor costs (as percentage)
- **Operating Margin:** Gross profit minus overhead (as percentage)
- **EBITDA / Net Income:** Earnings metrics (as applicable)
- **Burn Rate / Runway:** Cash consumption and months of operation (if not profitable)

**Purpose:**  
Assess financial sustainability and efficiency.

**Review Cadence:**  
- Monthly financial close
- Quarterly board reporting
- Annual budget and strategic plan

**Ownership:**  
- **Primary:** Finance (or Executive if no CFO)
- **Oversight:** Board

**Targets:**  
(Aligned with business model and capital structure; example: >30% gross margin, >15% operating margin)

### 2.4 Cash Flow & Collections

**Metrics:**
- **Days Sales Outstanding (DSO):** Average days to collect payment
- **Cash Balance:** Current cash and cash equivalents
- **Accounts Receivable Aging:** Outstanding invoices by age bucket
- **Payment Terms Compliance:** Percentage of clients paying within terms

**Purpose:**  
Monitor working capital and cash runway.

**Review Cadence:**  
- Weekly cash monitoring (if tight)
- Monthly financial close
- Quarterly board reporting

**Ownership:**  
- **Primary:** Finance (or Executive if no CFO)
- **Oversight:** Board

**Targets:**  
(Example: <45 days DSO, >90% collections within terms)

## 3. Talent & Team Metrics

### 3.1 Hiring & Growth

**Metrics:**
- **Headcount:** Total employees and contractors (by role)
- **Hiring Pipeline:** Candidates in process (by stage)
- **Time to Hire:** Days from job opening to offer acceptance
- **Offer Acceptance Rate:** Percentage of offers accepted

**Purpose:**  
Track team growth and hiring effectiveness.

**Review Cadence:**  
- Weekly hiring pipeline review
- Monthly headcount and growth tracking
- Quarterly board reporting

**Ownership:**  
- **Primary:** Hiring Manager / HR (if exists)
- **Rollup:** Executive Leadership
- **Oversight:** Board

**Targets:**  
(Aligned with hiring plan and delivery capacity needs)

### 3.2 Retention & Attrition

**Metrics:**
- **Voluntary Attrition Rate:** Percentage of employees leaving voluntarily (annualized)
- **Involuntary Attrition Rate:** Terminations for performance or conduct
- **Average Tenure:** Mean time employees have been with company
- **Key Person Risk:** Concentration of critical knowledge/relationships in individuals

**Purpose:**  
Monitor talent retention and key person dependencies.

**Review Cadence:**  
- Monthly attrition tracking
- Quarterly retention analysis and key person risk review
- Annual board review

**Ownership:**  
- **Primary:** Executive Leadership / HR (if exists)
- **Oversight:** Board

**Targets:**  
(Example: <15% voluntary attrition annually, no key person dependencies > 30%)

### 3.3 Team Development

**Metrics:**
- **Training Hours:** Hours invested in professional development (per engineer)
- **Certification Attainment:** Engineers earning relevant certifications
- **Performance Review Completion:** Percentage of team receiving timely reviews
- **Internal Promotions:** Engineers promoted from within vs. external hires

**Purpose:**  
Assess investment in team development and career growth.

**Review Cadence:**  
- Quarterly development tracking
- Annual board review

**Ownership:**  
- **Primary:** Delivery Leadership / HR (if exists)
- **Rollup:** Executive Leadership
- **Oversight:** Board

**Targets:**  
(Example: >20 hours training per engineer annually, >80% performance reviews on time)

## 4. Client & Pipeline Metrics

### 4.1 Client Acquisition

**Metrics:**
- **Lead Volume:** Inbound and outbound leads per period
- **Lead-to-Opportunity Conversion:** Percentage of leads becoming qualified opportunities
- **Opportunity-to-Win Rate:** Percentage of qualified opportunities won
- **Sales Cycle Length:** Days from initial contact to signed contract

**Purpose:**  
Monitor business development effectiveness and pipeline health.

**Review Cadence:**  
- Weekly pipeline review
- Monthly executive review
- Quarterly board reporting

**Ownership:**  
- **Primary:** Business Development / Sales
- **Rollup:** Executive Leadership
- **Oversight:** Board

**Targets:**  
(Based on market conditions and sales capacity; example: 20-30% win rate, <90 day sales cycle)

### 4.2 Client Concentration

**Metrics:**
- **Revenue Concentration:** Percentage of revenue from top 1, 3, 5 clients
- **Client Diversification:** Number of active clients and new clients per period
- **Client Lifetime Value:** Total revenue from client relationship over time
- **Client Churn Rate:** Percentage of clients not returning for additional work

**Purpose:**  
Monitor client concentration risk and relationship health.

**Review Cadence:**  
- Quarterly concentration analysis
- Annual board review

**Ownership:**  
- **Primary:** Business Development / Finance
- **Rollup:** Executive Leadership
- **Oversight:** Board

**Targets:**  
(Example: No single client >30% of revenue, <15% annual client churn)

### 4.3 Geographic & Industry Mix

**Metrics:**
- **Revenue by Geography:** Percentage from India, US, Europe, other
- **Revenue by Industry:** Percentage from healthcare, financial services, SaaS, etc.
- **Engagement Type Mix:** Percentage from T&M, fixed-price, retainer, staff aug

**Purpose:**  
Monitor diversification and strategic focus alignment.

**Review Cadence:**  
- Quarterly mix analysis
- Annual strategic planning

**Ownership:**  
- **Primary:** Business Development / Finance
- **Rollup:** Executive Leadership
- **Oversight:** Board

**Targets:**  
(Aligned with strategic focus; example: >40% US revenue, >30% healthcare/financial services)

## 5. Risk & Compliance Metrics

### 5.1 Security & Incidents

**Metrics:**
- **Security Incidents:** Number and severity of security events
- **Data Breaches:** Client data compromises or unauthorized access (should be zero)
- **Vulnerability Remediation Time:** Days to patch critical vulnerabilities
- **Security Training Completion:** Percentage of team completing security training

**Purpose:**  
Monitor security posture and incident response effectiveness.

**Review Cadence:**  
- Real-time incident monitoring
- Monthly security review
- Quarterly board reporting

**Ownership:**  
- **Primary:** Technical Leadership / Security Lead (if designated)
- **Rollup:** Executive Leadership
- **Oversight:** Board

**Targets:**  
(Example: Zero data breaches, <7 days critical vulnerability remediation)

### 5.2 Legal & Contractual

**Metrics:**
- **Contract Disputes:** Number of active disputes or legal issues with clients
- **SLA Breaches:** Violations of service level commitments (if applicable)
- **NDA/IP Violations:** Confidentiality or IP ownership issues
- **Insurance Coverage:** Liability, E&O, cyber insurance limits and renewal status

**Purpose:**  
Monitor legal and contractual risk exposure.

**Review Cadence:**  
- Real-time dispute monitoring
- Quarterly legal and compliance review
- Annual board review

**Ownership:**  
- **Primary:** Executive Leadership / Legal Counsel
- **Oversight:** Board

**Targets:**  
(Example: Zero active disputes, adequate insurance coverage)

### 5.3 Regulatory & Compliance

**Metrics:**
- **Regulatory Inquiries:** Requests or audits from regulatory bodies
- **Client Audits:** Number and outcome of client security/compliance audits
- **Certification Status:** Progress toward ISO, SOC 2, or other certifications (if pursued)
- **Data Processing Agreements:** Percentage of applicable engagements with DPAs/BAAs

**Purpose:**  
Monitor regulatory compliance and audit preparedness.

**Review Cadence:**  
- Quarterly compliance review
- Annual board review

**Ownership:**  
- **Primary:** Executive Leadership / Compliance Lead (if designated)
- **Oversight:** Board

**Targets:**  
(Example: Zero regulatory violations, 100% DPA/BAA coverage for GDPR/HIPAA contexts)

## 7. Reporting Structure & Accountability

### Management Reporting

**Weekly Operating Review (Executive Leadership):**
- Pipeline and sales activity
- Delivery status and escalations
- Utilization and staffing
- Critical issues and risks

**Monthly Operating Review (Executive Leadership):**
- Financial results vs. plan
- Key metrics dashboard (delivery, quality, hiring, client satisfaction)
- Risk and compliance status
- Strategic initiatives progress

**Quarterly Business Review (Executive + Board if exists):**
- Financial and operational performance
- Strategic initiatives and progress
- Risk assessment and mitigation
- Market and competitive insights
- Talent and team health

**Annual Strategic Planning (Executive + Board if exists):**
- Strategic direction and priorities
- Financial plan and targets
- Hiring and growth plan
- Capital needs and structure

## Conclusion

This Operating Metrics & Controls Snapshot provides the **structure** for monitoring business health, delivery quality, and risk. Actual data and performance against targets are reported separately in management and board reviews.

Key principles:
- **Metrics serve decisions:** Track what matters for management and board oversight
- **Accountability is clear:** Every metric has an owner and review cadence
- **Quality over volume:** Better to track fewer metrics accurately than many poorly
- **Continuous improvement:** Framework evolves as business matures
