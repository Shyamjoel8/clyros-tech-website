## Document Inventory

### 1. Clyros_Tech_Sales_Overview.md
**Type:** Sales presentation deck (content only)  
**Use Case:** New client meetings, partnership discussions, initial sales conversations  
**Format:** Markdown formatted as 10 slides  
**Audience:** CXOs, Technology Leadership, Business Development  

**Contents:**
- Company overview and positioning
- Service capabilities
- Industries served
- Delivery approach
- Representative work (anonymized)
- Trust and compliance
- Engagement models
- Contact information

**Usage:**
- Convert to PowerPoint/Google Slides for presentations
- Adapt specific slides for targeted pitches
- Use as baseline for customized sales decks

---

### 2. Clyros_Tech_RFP_Master.md
**Type:** Master RFP response content library  
**Use Case:** RFP responses, SOW development, due diligence questionnaires  
**Format:** Structured Markdown with reusable sections  
**Audience:** Procurement, Legal, Security, Compliance, Technical Evaluators  

**Contents:**
- Company overview and legal information
- Service capabilities (detailed)
- Delivery methodology
- Security practices
- Data privacy and protection
- Compliance and risk management
- Team structure and governance
- Relevant experience (anonymized)
- Commercial and engagement models

**Usage:**
- Copy relevant sections into RFP response documents
- Adapt language to match specific RFP questions
- Supplement with engagement-specific details
- Maintain consistency across all RFP responses

---

### 3. Clyros_Tech_Engagement_Models.md
**Type:** Engagement models one-pager  
**Use Case:** Commercial discussions, procurement conversations, contract scoping  
**Format:** Structured Markdown one-pager  
**Audience:** CXOs, Procurement, Finance, Project Management  

**Contents:**
- Overview of engagement philosophy
- Four engagement types (fixed-scope, dedicated teams, managed services, hybrid)
- Typical timelines
- Client and vendor responsibilities
- Governance and communication cadence
- Risk mitigation approach
- Getting started process

**Usage:**
- Share with prospects during commercial discussions
- Attach to proposals as engagement model overview
- Use in contract scoping conversations
- Reference during governance setup

## Usage Guidelines

### Internal Use
✅ **Permitted:**
- Use in sales presentations and client meetings
- Adapt for specific opportunities and RFPs
- Share with internal teams for alignment
- Customize for specific industries or use cases

❌ **Prohibited:**
- Do not make unauthorized claims or promises
- Do not contradict website or official materials
- Do not share client-identifiable information without approval
- Do not commit to capabilities not yet available

### External Sharing
✅ **Permitted:**
- Share engagement models document with prospects
- Include RFP master content in proposals (adapted)
- Convert sales deck to presentation format for client meetings

❌ **Prohibited:**
- Do not share raw Markdown files externally
- Do not share without appropriate branding and formatting
- Do not share under NDA without legal review
- Do not include confidential internal notes or pricing

### Customization
When adapting content for specific opportunities:
- Maintain tone and positioning consistency
- Do not inflate or exaggerate capabilities
- Ensure all claims are supportable and accurate
- Have customized content reviewed before client submission
