## 10. Commercial & Engagement Models

### 10.1 Engagement Types

**Fixed-Scope Projects**
- Well-defined requirements and deliverables
- Fixed timeline and budget
- Milestone-based payment terms
- Change order process for scope changes
- Ideal for: Specific initiatives with clear requirements

**Time and Materials**
- Hourly or daily rate structure
- Flexible scope and timeline
- Monthly invoicing based on effort
- Ideal for: Discovery, advisory, variable-scope work

**Dedicated Engineering Teams**
- Monthly team allocation (one or more engineers)
- Quarterly or annual commitment options
- Flexible capacity scaling
- Team embedded within client organization
- Ideal for: Sustained product development, capacity augmentation

**Managed Services**
- SLA-based service delivery
- Monthly fixed fee or consumption-based pricing
- 24/7 support coverage (where applicable)
- Performance metrics and reporting
- Ideal for: Production support, ongoing operations

**Hybrid Engagements**
Custom structures combining project delivery, team augmentation, and managed services based on client needs.
