## PAGE 4: HOW WE WORK

### Headline
**Proven Methodology. Predictable Outcomes.**

### Our Delivery Approach

**Discovery & Planning**  
We invest time understanding your business context, technical landscape, regulatory environment, and organizational dynamics before proposing solutions.

**Architecture & Design**  
We favor proven architectures over experimental ones. Every design decision is documented with rationale, constraints, and trade-offs clearly explained.

**Iterative Development**  
Work is structured in manageable increments with clear acceptance criteria. Progress is transparent. Risks are escalated early.

**Quality Assurance**  
Testing is integrated throughout—not a final-phase activity. Security, performance, and compliance validation occur continuously.

**Knowledge Transfer**  
Documentation is created during delivery, not after. Your team is embedded in decision-making and equipped to maintain what we build.

**Production Support**  
We remain engaged through launch and stabilization. Runbooks, monitoring, and escalation procedures are established before we transition out.

## DESIGN NOTES FOR YOU

### Layout Suggestions
- **Page 1 (Cover):** Full-page visual with centered text
- **Page 2 (Who We Are):** Two-column layout with pull quotes
- **Page 3 (Services):** Grid layout for service categories
- **Page 4 (How We Work):** Timeline or process flow visual
- **Page 5 (Why Us + Contact):** Split layout with CTA emphasis

### Typography Hierarchy
- **Headlines:** Bold, large (32-48pt)
- **Subheads:** Semibold, medium (18-24pt)
- **Body:** Regular, readable (11-14pt)
- **Captions:** Light, small (9-11pt)

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Navy Blue (#1e3a8a)
- **Secondary:** Slate Gray (#64748b)
- **Background:** White / Light Gray (#f8fafc)
- **Accent:** Electric Blue (#3b82f6) - use sparingly

### Visual Elements
- Clean geometric patterns
- Professional photography (avoid stock handshake images)
- Icons for service categories (line style, not filled)
- Generous whitespace (don't cram)
- Subtle borders/dividers

### Font Recommendations
- **Primary:** Inter, Helvetica Neue, or similar sans-serif
- **Avoid:** Script fonts, decorative fonts, anything "creative"
